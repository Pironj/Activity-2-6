Keeshonden

Keeshonden are known for being companion animals. They were originally Dutch barge dogs. They have a very high-pitch bark that were used to pierce the fog and alert other ships. The Keeshond was thought to be a spiritual animal for the native people protecting them when on the water.

Fun fact! The Keeshond's nickname is "The Smiling Dutchman". Why?..because they leave their mouths open often when looking at you. They often have dark counter shading on their coat that gives them the look of a 5-O'clock shadow, which enhances the smile!

Real talk - Their bark can be very annoying
